\noindent \textbf{Question:} A uniform charge distribution with charge density $\rho_v=-\dfrac{3}{2\pi}$ C/m$^3$ exists in the region $r<a$. This spherical charge distribution is enclosed in a conducting shell from $r=b$ to $r=c$. Surface charge density on the outer surface of conductor is $\rho_S=\dfrac{1}{36\pi}$ C/m$^2$. Here $a=1$ m, $b=2$ m and $c=3$ m.
\begin{itemize}
\item[a.] Draw a figure depicting the geometry of this problem.
\item[b.] Calculate net charge on conductor.
\item[c.] Find electric field everywhere.
\end{itemize}
