\noindent\textbf{Question 1} A wire of radius $0.1$ cm carries $5$ mA current. Find \textbf{H}--field everywhere.\\[0.2cm]
\noindent\textbf{Question 2} A parallel--plate capacitor with plate area of 10 cm$^2$ and plate separation of 1 mm has an electric field $\textbf{E}=12\sin 1000t$ V/m applied across its plates. If the capacitor dielectric is $\epsilon=5\epsilon_0$ find the displacement current flowing through the capacitor.\\[0.2cm]
\noindent\textbf{Question 3} A toroid has a core with mean radius $\rho_0$ = 10 cm and a circular cross--sectional radius $a=1$ cm. If the core is made of steel ($\mu = 1000\mu_0$) and has a coil with 200 turns, calculate the amount of current that will produce a flux of 1 mWb in the core. Assume magnetic flux in the toroid is constant by taking $\rho_0>>a$.
