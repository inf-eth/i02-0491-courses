\noindent \textbf{Question:} Given a vector field
\begin{equation*}
\textbf{A}=(1-y)\hat x+(2x-z)\hat y+z^2\hat z
\end{equation*}
a. Find and sketch the field vector at following points in the $xy$--plane.
\begin{enumerate}
\item[(1)] (0, 1, 0)
\item[(2)] (1, 1, 0)
\item[(3)] (-1, 0, 0)
\item[(4)] (-1, -1, 0)
\end{enumerate}
b. Find magnitude of the field at (-1.1, 2.3, 1.2).
