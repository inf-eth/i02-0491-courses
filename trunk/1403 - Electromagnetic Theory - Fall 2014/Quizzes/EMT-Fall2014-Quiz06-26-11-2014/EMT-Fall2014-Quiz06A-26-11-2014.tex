\noindent \textbf{Question:} A semi--circular disk of inner radius $\rho=a$ and outer radius $\rho=b$ has thickness $t$ and conductivity $\sigma$. Find the resistance between the ends of the disk $\phi=0$ and $\phi=\pi$. Laplace equation in cylindrical coordinates is $\nabla^2V=\dfrac{1}{\rho}\dfrac{\partial}{\partial\rho}\left(\rho\dfrac{\partial V}{\partial\rho}\right)+\dfrac{1}{\rho^2}\dfrac{\partial^2V}{\partial\phi^2}+\dfrac{\partial^2V}{\partial z^2}=0$.
