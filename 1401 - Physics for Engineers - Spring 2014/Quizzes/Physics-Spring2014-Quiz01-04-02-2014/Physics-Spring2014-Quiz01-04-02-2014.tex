\vspace{9cm}

\noindent \textbf{Question 2:} Given
\begin{equation*}
\begin{split}
&\textbf{A}=3\hat x-2\hat y+4\hat z\\
&\textbf{B}=-\hat x+4\hat y+2\hat z\\
&\textbf{C}=-5\hat x+5\hat y
\end{split}
\end{equation*}
Find values of $\alpha$ and $\beta$ such that, $\alpha\textbf{A}+\beta\textbf{B}+\textbf{C}$ is parallel to $y$--axis.
